About vibeBox

Hey there! vibeBox started as a collection of handy developer tools for the modern web, designed to make your specific dev and design tasks a bit smoother and your workflow more efficient. It's also a lightweight launchpad for "vibe coding" projects, offering a simple Docker Compose setup with example services to quickly get you started with a containerized development environment.

All tools are free to use, open-source, and built with privacy in mind. No data leaves your browser unless explicitly needed for the tool to function.
